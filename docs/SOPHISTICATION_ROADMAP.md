# Sophistication Roadmap

This public roadmap summarizes the major capability areas of ZenInvest without
including private launch planning or operator-only delivery notes.

## Delivered Themes

- multi-stage orchestration for research, moderation, risk, and execution
- dashboard and public-safe read surfaces
- agentic research tooling with budgets and traceability
- stop management, broker reconciliation, and execution hardening
- backtesting and validation tooling

## Active Themes

- continued trading hardening and execution quality improvements
- richer public-safe product documentation
- further dashboard and workflow polish
- improved validation, monitoring, and operator ergonomics

## Public Roadmap Use

Treat this document as a high-level direction signal. Detailed internal launch
plans, private operational milestones, and infrastructure rollout notes remain
outside the public mirror.
