Concept 08

Quantum Graph Z

The best of all four fused together. The Z is built from graph nodes and edges (#3's futuristic construction), floating inside an orbital hexagonal ring (#2's frame + gradient), with a denser mesh of cross-connections (#1's circuit energy), and a single white-core centre node (#5's minimal accent). The ring vertices connect to the Z's corners, suggesting a system that's both self-contained and connected to something larger. The violet→cyan→emerald gradient runs through everything.
